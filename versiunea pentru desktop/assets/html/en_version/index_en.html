Welcome!

The baccalaureate exam for many of the 12th grade graduates is a big inconvenience, especially if during high school they did not learn too much French. What do we do? We are trying to help you pass the oral C test in French. We have a series of exercises to help you with this and many other useful materials. Practice with us and watch how you progress!

French is a Indo-European language of the family of Romance languages, namely their western branch, where, in the group of Galoromanic languages, it is one of the oïl languages. French is speaking by a total of about 220 million people either as a mother tongue, as a second language or as a foreign language in the member countries, associated or observer status of the International Francophonie Organization, as well as in several other countries outside it, such as Algeria or the United States of America.

French words are present in many fields, especially in the fields of gastronomy, fashion, cosmetics or the army. English is the language that borrowed most of the French words. The English-language lexicon is, according to some authors, 30%, of French origin. Romanian is another language in which the number of words of French origin is relatively high: 22.12% of the lexicon.
